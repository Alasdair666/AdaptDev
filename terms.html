Your Rights to Re-Schedule or Cancel

If written notice is received:

  • With at least 30 business days notice the customer will be entitled to a full refund of their payment
  • For cancellations with greater notice than fourteen (14) business days but less than Thirty (30) business days notice of cancellation in advance of the class they will be entitled to a 50% refund
  • For cancellations with greater than seven (7) business days notice of cancellation but less than fourteen (14) days in advance of the class, the customer will only be eligible for a 25% refund of their payment amount or a full class credit that must be used for another class within six (6) months of the date of the original class
  • The customer is not entitled to a refund or class credit if less than seven (7) business days advance written notice is given
  • Failure to show up for class will result in forfeiture of all payment

Registrants who cancel will not receive training materials.
