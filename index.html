Professonal Scrum Product Owner

The Professional Scrum Product Owner (PSPO) course teaches people how to maximize the delivery of value through software products and systems.

PSPO covers the topics:

  • Value Driven Development
  • Product Management
  • Planning Releases
  • Lean Planning & Managing Products

Professional Scrum Master

The Professional Scrum Master (PSM) course is THE cutting-edge course for effective Scrum Masters and anyone supporting a software development team's efficiency and effectiveness.

PSM covers the topics:

  • Scrum Framework
  • Scrum Theory and Principles
  • Cross-functional, Self-organizing teams Coaching & Facilitation
  • Scrum at Large

Professional Scrum Developer (PSD)

The PSD course teaches students how to work in a team, using modern software engineering practices and your specific technology platform to develop an increment of potentially releasable functionality.

PSD covers 3 primary topics (below):

  • Scrum
  • Software development & ALM Tools
  • Engineering Practices
